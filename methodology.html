The engine

How the number is built — the same way for everyone.

The data is the fuel. The engine is what turns it into a grade you can trust — graded against the greatest, disciplined by design, and impossible to buy.

Graded against the greatest
Every athlete is scored relative to the best to ever play their sport — not curved against the kids next to them. The number means the same thing in every gym, every level, every sport.
Eight facets, one number
The read breaks into eight graded facets and synthesizes into a single 0–10. The shape is the evidence — the polygon shows exactly where the number comes from: four traits on the surface, the four invisible ones underneath.
AI reads the evidence; rules keep it honest
The model judges the film and the record; deterministic checks enforce consistency where a number can't drift. Judgment from the AI, discipline from the code — so the same player gets the same read every time.
One algorithm, every athlete
No hand-tuning a favorite. No cap bought. No grade curated. One engine, every level. The number is the number.
Evidence in, or no grade
A read is only as good as the signal behind it. Thin evidence earns a careful read, never a confident fake — which is exactly why more data makes every number sharper.

It learns

Every read makes the next one sharper.

FieldCheck isn't a static formula. Five loops feed back into the engine — most of them ones a pay-to-play site structurally can't run.

Cross-source agreement
When the record, the write-ups, and the film point the same way, confidence rises. When they disagree, we look closer.
Human corrections
A coach or athlete who disagrees with a read teaches the engine something real.
The upload flywheel
Honest reads pull players in; their clips make the next read better.
Usage signal
What coaches actually watch and share tells us which reads matter.
Outcome calibration — the long game the record is live
As athletes commit and reach their level, reality scores our read. Every read is now logged the day we make it — append-only, timestamped — so reality can grade us. See The Record → No accuracy claim yet; we publish that only when the outcomes back it. See the full loop we're building →
The agents

A team of agents — the labor recruiting charges for, free.

The read is the start. On top of it, FieldCheck runs the work a $3,000 service sells to anxious parents — done by AI, grounded in a grade that can't be inflated.
