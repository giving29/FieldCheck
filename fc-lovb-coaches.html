§ IV · The "Coach of Coaches" lens

How LOVB grows coaches.

LOVB's "Coach of Coaches" framework treats coach development as a primary pipeline. Below is our independent description of the framework and what makes it different from typical certification programs.

// Coach of Coaches · LOVB's framework, independently described

Mentorship, cross-tier exchange, certification.

The premise: a vertically integrated league can develop coaches the same way it develops athletes. Junior club coaches affiliated with LOVB get structured mentorship from Pro head coaches — including site visits, off-season convenings, and the LOVB Pro Academies cycles. Pro assistant coaches are explicit pipeline destinations for elite junior club coaches. The Coach of Coaches certification, run quarterly, is the formal credential layer. What we find most interesting is the cross-tier exchange itself — most pro leagues don't visibly train the youth coaches feeding their roster pipeline.
