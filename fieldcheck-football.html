§ III · Anchor pipelines

Where the data lives.

Football's data landscape is more fragmented than volleyball's. Multiple competing recruiting services, multiple competing combines, multiple pro pathways. Our anchor strategy: lean hard on publicly verifiable sources, never on paid recruiting rankings.

// PRIMARY · D1 FBS
The flagship pipeline.
134 D1 FBS programs. Roster pages, scholarship counts, depth charts, NFL Draft outcomes — all publicly indexed via team athletic sites. Strongest single anchor for college-side data.
// PRIMARY · NFL COMBINE
The measurables anchor.
40-yard dash, vertical, shuttle, broad jump, bench. Published openly for every Combine invitee. Provides the strongest historical archetype scaffold — same physical thresholds anchor each position year over year.
// SECONDARY · TRANSFER PORTAL
The mobility layer.
Modern football is portal-fluid. 1,800+ FBS scholarship players enter the portal annually. The destination graph is public and observable; it's a primary signal for archetype landing.
// SECONDARY · NFL DRAFT
The outcome anchor.
~260 picks annually. Position-by-pick mapping is publicly verifiable. Combined with Combine data, the draft outcome layer fully anchors the pro-tier archetype set.
// EXCLUDED · PAID RANKINGS
What we won't use.
247Sports rankings, Rivals stars, On3 NIL valuations. All paid commercial products. We'll cite their existence; we won't anchor scoring on them. Same stance volleyball took on recruiting services.
// EXCLUDED · NIL DEALS
Why we stay out.
NIL is too opaque and inconsistent to use as a pathway signal. We may surface large publicly disclosed deals as facts; we won't use NIL valuation as an archetype input.
§ IV · Archetype skeleton

What an archetype looks like.

Each position has a small set of archetype "types" anchored on visible career arcs. Below is a sample skeleton — the QB archetypes that would ship in the first football release.

// QB · Archetype 1
The Pocket Surgeon
Statue in the pocket. Elite arm strength + processing speed. Production driven by play action and rhythm passing. Career arc anchor: Burrow, Stafford, Mahomes (early-career frame).
// QB · Archetype 2
The Dual-Threat Conductor
Plus mobility. Designed runs and second-reaction throws. Production driven by RPO and extending plays. Career arc anchor: Lamar, Daniels, Hurts, Mahomes (late-career frame).
// QB · Archetype 3
The Game-Manager Steady
Functional arm. Decision-making over physical tools. Production driven by limiting turnovers and capitalizing on defense. Career arc anchor: Cousins, Goff, Tagovailoa.
// QB · Archetype 4
The Athlete-First Project
Elite physical tools. Technical refinement is the bottleneck. Outcomes wide: HOF if it clicks, fast washout if not. Career arc anchor: Allen (early), Fields, Lance.
