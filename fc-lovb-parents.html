§ I · What it actually costs

The cost ranges, no marketing math.

Annual cost bands for a competitive club volleyball season, based on publicly published tuition pages across LOVB-affiliated and other clubs in the U.S. 2024–2026. Ranges, not averages, because your geography matters more than any club's average.

// Regional level
12U–14U Regional
$1.5K – $3.5K
Local tournaments only. 2–3 practices/week, no national travel. Entry point for early competitive play.
// National qualifier
15U–17U Open
$5K – $10K
National travel. 4–5 practices/week, 4–6 out-of-state tournaments, qualifier circuit fees. The middle of the curve.
// Elite national
17U / 18U Open
$8K – $15K
Full national + dual-org schedule. USAV nationals + AAU nationals + qualifier circuit. Often the cost of in-state tuition.
// Per-family budget
Travel · Lodging · Apparel
+$3K – $8K
Above and beyond tuition. Flights, hotels, rental cars, team apparel, club gear. Doubles as a logistics workload — not just a cost.
!
The compounding problem. Multiply mid-curve ($8K) × six club years (12U–17U) = $48K just-club costs over a typical pathway. Add travel, lodging, apparel, missed work — true cost approaches $80K–$120K per family. The LOVB Foundation exists specifically to widen that filter for families who can't underwrite it.

§ IV · Choosing a club

A checklist your club won't hand you.

If you're evaluating clubs for next season — LOVB-affiliated or not — these are the questions worth asking before you sign tuition. Real questions, asked of real people, evaluated against real data.

// Parent due diligence · The 8-question filter

Eight questions before you sign.

  • Show me your alumni outcomes. Not your "verbal commits." A list of where actual graduates went and what they're doing now. If they can't produce it within 24 hours, that's signal.
  • Who's the head coach for my daughter's team? Not the program. Her specific team. What's their pathway? Are they returning next year?
  • What's the practice-to-tournament ratio? A team that practices 5x/week but plays 3 tournaments all season produces different athletes than one that plays 8 tournaments. Match to your daughter's growth need.
  • What's the actual all-in cost? Tuition + travel + nationals fees + apparel + qualifier fees. Get it in writing. "~$X" is not a number.
  • How are playing-time decisions made? Is it explicit? Performance-based? Rotation-based? Parents don't get to choose, but they deserve to know the framework.
  • What's the relationship with HS volleyball? Some clubs accommodate; some compete. Check whether club practice schedules conflict with HS programs in your district.
  • What happens if my daughter wants to quit mid-season? Refund policy. Re-entry policy. Many parents discover this answer too late.
  • Is there scholarship support? If you're going to need help, ask early. LOVB-affiliated clubs have Foundation channels; others may not.
§ V · The promise to parents

What we won't do.

Most "parent resources" sites are funded by recruiting services, club networks, or club-cert providers. Ours isn't. Here's what that means concretely.

// Our parent pledge · No exceptions

Six commitments. In writing.

  • No emails to your daughter. If she fills in the Pathway tool, we don't send her marketing. Ever.
  • No data resale. If she submits a profile, that data does not go to recruiting services, agents, college coaches, or anyone else.
  • No club fees. No LOVB-affiliated club, no non-affiliated club, no club anywhere pays to be highlighted in our directory or ranked higher. We don't rank.
  • No "premium" tier. Everything described here is free. There's no "Plus" version that unlocks more. Ever.
  • No inflated probabilities. The Pathway tool will not tell your daughter she "can be a star." It will tell her what the data actually sees.
  • Walk-away default. Every recommendation supports declining as a valid choice. If the answer is "this club isn't worth it for your family," that's the answer.
