Why we're not publishing
a price sheet yet.

We're building something that goes well beyond what any recruiting service, ranking platform, or scouting tool has done. The right pricing model for that isn't obvious — and we'd rather get it right than rush it.

Some intelligence will always be free. The verdict, the five-layer read, the signed audit — these build trust and that trust needs to be accessible. The deeper layers, the institutional tools, the real-time data pipelines — those take serious resources to build and maintain, and they'll be priced accordingly.

If you're a program, a scout, a league, or a serious individual user — reach out. We're having those conversations now.
