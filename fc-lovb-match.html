§ 02 · Methodology
InputsPosition, height, optional approach jump, career arc descriptor, free-text style keywords.
MatchingThe engine computes a weighted similarity score against each of the ten Founding Athletes. Position match counts most. Physical profile distance is the second-largest factor. Career arc and free-text style keywords act as multipliers.
Style keywordsStyle descriptors (power, quick, tempo, defensive, finisher, vocal, calm, etc.) are token-matched against each Founding Athlete's documented style profile. Match strength scales with overlap.
Why match-not-rankThis tool answers "who plays like this" — not "how good is she." For projection ranking use Pro Fit Score. Both tools should agree on the directional read; if they don't, the inputs are inconsistent.
LimitsFounding Athlete profiles are summaries. Live LOVB rosters include many talented athletes beyond the Founding Ten; for those comparisons use /lovb/athletes. A future tool expansion will include all rostered pros.
