§ 02 · Methodology
InputsPosition, playing style, projection tier, optional region and market-size preference, and a comfort-with-competition signal.
Market profilesEach of the six pro markets has a documented profile: positional need pattern, system style (e.g. quick-tempo vs power), coaching style, market size, regional location, and current roster depth at each position.
ScoringThe fit score is a weighted average of: positional need × 30%, system style match × 25%, projection tier × roster depth (does the team need elite or developmental at this position) × 20%, geography × 10%, market size × 8%, competition comfort × 7%.
What it answers"Where does she most naturally fit?" — not "where will she be signed." Free agency, contract economics, and personal relationships dominate the actual signing path. This tool predicts the basketball-fit-for-volleyball answer; the rest of the decision lives outside the model.
LimitsRoster depth profiles are point-in-time. As trades and re-signings happen, the same player profile may score differently across the same six markets. Treat the output as a snapshot, not a forecast.
