About this archive. And why it matters.

The archive is the publication surface for FieldCheck's strategic and methodological work. Every document is versioned, stably-URL'd, and citable. The CC BY-SA 4.0 license makes adoption — by coaches, media, college programs, academic researchers — explicitly unimpeded.

The strategic premise: when a methodology is published, defended, and used by enough independent parties, it becomes infrastructure. Bloomberg Terminal energy, not blog energy. Standards are the deepest moat in any information business.

Disputes, comments, or citation queries: methodology@fieldcheck.ai.
