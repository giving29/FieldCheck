Every available public source. School rosters. Governing-body archives. NIL marketplaces. Tournament records. Live news. Verified video. Reconciled to a single canonical record.

// What greatness sounds like

"The standard is the standard. We don't lower it for anybody. We expect everyone to play to that standard."

— Mike Tomlin · NFL head coach

"Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work hard."

— Tim Notke · high school basketball coach (often credited to Kevin Durant)

"Don't tell me what you're going to do. Don't tell me what you've done. Show me."

— Nick Saban · 7-time national champion head coach

"You miss 100% of the shots you don't take."

— Wayne Gretzky · widely attributed

"Excellence is the gradual result of always striving to do better."

— Pat Riley · 5-time NBA champion coach

"I've missed more than 9,000 shots in my career. I've lost almost 300 games. 26 times I've been trusted to take the game-winning shot and missed. I've failed over and over and over again — and that is why I succeed."

— Michael Jordan · widely attributed (Nike "Failure" ad, 1997)

"Hardships often prepare ordinary people for an extraordinary destiny."

— C.S. Lewis · widely cited in athletics
