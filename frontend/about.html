// WHY WE EXIST

Every athlete deserves
the truth about the path.

We built FieldCheck so a parent in a small town can see the same independent read on a recruit as the GM at a power-conference program. So a coach can verify what a kid actually did — not what a service got paid to claim. So a sixteen-year-old chasing the cut knows exactly what it takes — and what it doesn't.

No ads. No payola. No pay-to-rank. The verdict, on the record.
// FOUR PRINCIPLES

Built on a trust pledge.

01
No ads.
No banner ads, no sponsored verdicts, no "featured" placements. Nothing in a FieldCheck verdict was paid for.
02
No payola.
No program, agent, parent, or third party can pay to influence a tier, a score, or a recommendation. We have no business model that depends on it.
03
No pay-to-rank.
Recruiting services have a long history of selling rankings. We refuse. Tiers are derived from public record + curated subjective read, both auditable.
04
On the record.
Every verdict ships with a signed audit JSON URL. Every claim traces to a source. Disputes route to a human in 5 business days.
// HOW WE BUILD

The receipts make the claim.

Independence isn't a slogan; it's a mechanism. Three pieces hold it together.

01 · CURATED DATABASE
Hand-verified marquee profiles.
For top-tier names, we maintain hand-curated profiles with real measurables, real production stats, and a subjective tier read. Generic LLMs hallucinate at this level. We publish the source.
02 · AUDIT JSON
Every claim traces to a source.
Every verdict ships with a signed JSON URL containing the inputs, the sources, and the reasoning. No closed-box scoring. You can read what we read.
03 · DISPUTE PIPELINE
A human responds in 5 business days.
If a verdict is wrong — wrong stat, wrong source, wrong read — email dispute@fieldcheck.ai. A human reviews and either updates or explains, on a 5-business-day SLA.

LAYER 1 · THE VOICES

16 individuals per sport. Real friction. The signal.

Per sport — 16 Voices (player × parent × coach × scout, across ICON / ELITE / PROSPECT / STAR tiers). Each is one real person, not a deliberative body. If we can't name which Voice asked for a feature, we don't ship it.

No committees. No standing review meetings. Their friction is the roadmap — direct, unfiltered.

LAYER 2 · OPERATING BRAINS

Four specialist agents. On-call. In concert.

CMO Brain, CFO Brain, Chief Data & Analytics Brain, COO Brain — each activated per-decision, not standing. When a marketing question is in front of us, the CMO Brain shows up. When the question's done, it steps aside. They work in concert as needed, never as a committee.

They never override product decisions. The Voices drive the path. The Brains help run the company that builds the path.
