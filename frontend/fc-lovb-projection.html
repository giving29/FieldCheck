§ 02 · Methodology
InputsPosition, height, kills/set (or position-equivalent), hitting %, conference strength, year of eligibility, honors, postseason exposure.
WeightsPosition-specific. OH/OPP weight hitting % and kills/set highest. MB weights blocking proxies (hitting % and height). S weights assist/set and tournament exposure. L weights digs/set and postseason performance.
Tiers80+ First-round pro · 65–79 Second-round / likely pro · 50–64 Stash / development · < 50 Not yet — keep developing or transfer first.
ConfidenceInterval widens for freshmen/sophomores (smaller sample) and narrows for seniors with NCAA postseason tape. Mid-major projections carry a higher uncertainty band than power-conference players with the same raw stats.
LimitsThis is a projection tool, not a verdict. It uses publicly available statistical patterns. It does not replace coach evaluation, video study, or athlete interviews. Use it as a starting point — not a destination.
SourcingPosition thresholds calibrated against the current LOVB Pro roster as published at lovb.com. Conference strength weights derived from NCAA postseason representation patterns. Full methodology and citations: /lovb/sources.
