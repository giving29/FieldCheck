Partners.

FieldCheck pathways for programs, agents, and media. The public side stays free forever. Programs that want bulk evaluation, custom cohort review, or embedded verdict badges follow the partner pathway.

For programs (coaches, ADs, recruiting offices)

  • Bulk roster eval · upload roster CSV; get 8-facet verdicts on every athlete, source-cited, in a shareable program dashboard.
  • Trajectory monitoring · alerts when a roster athlete moves tiers or shows composite drift in either direction.
  • Recruit class evaluation · the same scoring framework applied to commits + targets, including HS-amateur-cap doctrine to keep ceiling assumptions honest.
  • Comparable program benchmarking · how does our roster's composite distribution stack against the conference?

When this ships: Phase 11, Q3 2026. Early-access for programs willing to be calibration partners during the cohort-expansion window.

For agents + management

  • NIL stress-test · how does this athlete's NIL valuation align with their FieldCheck composite + tier?
  • Trajectory probabilities · forward bands on draft positioning, contract leverage, breakout risk.
  • Comparable client benchmarking · how does your client's evaluation compare to peers in the same tier + position?

When this ships: Phase 12, Q3 2026.

For media + content partners

  • Embedded verdict badges · drop the official FieldCheck composite/tier onto any article. Live updates when calibration ships.
  • White-listed citation · use the eval grid + source attribution in your reporting. Free, no permission needed, just attribute.
  • Cohort exclusives · early access to upcoming cohort drops 7 days before public ship, for credentialed press only.
What doesn't change Partner pathways don't influence the verdict. No pay-for-grade, ever. The PhD panel reviews evaluations independent of any commercial relationship. This is the line the prediction-ledger commits us to holding.

Get in touch
