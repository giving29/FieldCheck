★ The principle

The composite scale is a single universe spanning all human athletic achievement — gender-blind, age-blind, sport-blind (UTR model). 10.0 is mathematically unreachable; nobody sits there. 9 is vapor: active athletes cap below 9.3 until career complete. Every decimal earned = dozens-to-hundreds of athletes passed. Pro-context facets (character under pro pressure, mental strength in playoff crucibles, coachability under pro coaching, competitiveness vs pros) cannot have evidence at lower tiers — so HS players are hard-capped by missing facet evidence.

★ Why this works · the facet ceiling logic

The composite is a weighted average of 8 facets. A facet cannot score higher than the evidence supports. At HS, even generational talents like LeBron at 18 would weighted-avg ~6.5-6.8 because:
  • Talent facet 9 (against HS competition, proven) ✓
  • Physical facet 8 (pro-ready frame, rare for HS) ✓
  • Mental IQ facet 6 (HS game IQ, unproven vs college/pro)
  • Character facet 6 (HS pressure context only — no pro-pressure evidence)
  • Mindset facet 6 (still forming)
  • Mental strength facet 5-6 (no playoff crucible evidence)
  • Coachability facet 6 (HS coaching, very different from pro)
  • Competitiveness facet 7 (vs HS competition only)
Weighted = ~6.5. Tyran Stokes with elite talent but unproven everything-else = 5.3. Jordan 1996 with 9-10 in every facet across multi-year multi-context proof = 9.9.
