Privacy

Privacy Policy.

Last updated: June 9 2026

FieldCheck IQ ("we," "us," "FieldCheck") respects your privacy. This policy describes what we collect, why, and your rights. Plain language; no dark patterns.

What we collect from visitors

The public verdict pages and informational pages (methodology, predictions, coverage) work without any personal data. Specifically:

  • No login required for any public content.
  • No third-party trackers. No Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no marketing pixels.
  • localStorage only. Your watchlist, tier history, and dismissed alerts live in your browser. We don't sync these to a server.
  • Server logs. Cloudflare and Netlify record standard access logs (IP, user-agent, timestamp, requested URL) for operational reliability. These rotate on a 30-day window.

What we collect from people who contact us

If you submit a contact form, email us, or sign up for tier-jump alerts:

  • Email address you provide (only for alert delivery + response).
  • Athlete subscription preferences (which athletes you want notifications about).
  • Message content you wrote.

Forms are processed by Netlify Forms. Subscriber data lives in Netlify's database. We never sell, rent, or share this data.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell data. Ever.
  • We don't display third-party ads.
  • We don't share data with athlete agents, programs, or commercial partners.
  • We don't track you across other sites.
  • We don't profile you for commercial purposes.

Cookies

The site uses no first-party cookies. Cloudflare and Netlify set their own technical cookies (security + load balancing); these are not for tracking.

About athletes evaluated on FieldCheck

FieldCheck verdicts are based on publicly available information — official school athletic site pages, public stats, public interviews, public NIL records, public press coverage. Photos are sourced from publicly available og:image metadata on the same sources.

If you are an athlete (or guardian of an athlete) and want a verdict corrected or removed, see the dispute path on any verdict page or contact us via the form. Disputed cases are reviewed by the PhD panel; re-grades are versioned and public.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you (subject to operational log retention).
  • Opt out of tier-jump alerts at any time (unsubscribe link in every email).
  • Clear your local watchlist + tier history (button in Watchlist).

Children

FieldCheck does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. Athletes evaluated may be under 18 (HS recruits); however, this is based on publicly available information about their athletic performance, not personal data we collect from them.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us through any verdict page contact form with subject "Privacy". We respond within 48 hours.
