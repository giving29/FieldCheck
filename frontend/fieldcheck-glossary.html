Glossary · the language behind the verdict

Glossary.

Every term used in a FieldCheck verdict, defined precisely. No fluff, no marketing — the vocabulary as used in the methodology.

Composite
The 0–10 weighted average across all 8 facets. The single number that maps to the tier ladder. Higher is better.
Tier
Composite mapped to a label: ICON (9.5–10), ELITE+ (9.0–9.4), ELITE (7.5–8.9), STAR (7.0–7.4), PROSPECT (5.5–6.9), SCOUT (3.5–5.4).
Facet
One of 8 canonical evaluation dimensions: Character · Mindset · Mental Strength · Talent · Physical · Mental/IQ · Coachability · Competitiveness. Same 8 across every sport, weights vary.
Eval Grid
The full per-facet scoring block on every verdict page. Shows each facet's score + evidence summary + source citations.
Cohort
A versioned batch of locked verdicts. The current cohort is v1.7.A (locked May 17 2026 with 50 pre-registered predictions).
Calibration
The set of weights + thresholds that turn raw evidence into scores. Currently V5.5 doctrine (locked June 8 2026). Calibration changes are versioned; all prior verdicts re-score on a calibration version bump.
Amateur Cap
The doctrinal constraint that HS amateurs can't score above SCOUT (5.4) and college freshmen can't score above STAR (7.4). Protects against ceiling-visibility errors that the rest of the market routinely makes.
Polish Set
The hand-curated set of marquee athletes (currently ~30) with full eval grids, source-cited evidence, and verified photos. The "we did the work" tier.
Hidden Gem
A cross-cohort discovery match — a lower-tier athlete whose composite is unexpectedly close to a higher-tier athlete in another sport or position. Surfaced on verdict pages via the FCBase45 card.
Trajectory
The forward-projection band shown on each verdict — where we expect the athlete's composite to land at the next major checkpoint (typically 18 months out).
Peer
An athlete in the same tier, same sport, and same position whose composite is within ±0.3 of the subject. Shown in the Peers strip.
Tier-Jump Alert
The purple banner shown when a watched athlete moves up a tier between visits. Surfaces on the FCBase53 frontend.
Verdict
The single page representing a graded athlete. Includes hero, eval grid, peers, hidden-gem matches, sources, dispute path.
PhD Panel
25 named PhD reviewers (anonymized publicly for now) who vetted the framework + cohort verdicts. Reviews weekly (Patel), monthly (full panel), quarterly (weight updates), annually (refinements), 5-yearly (major versions).
Tenet
An operating principle locked into the build. Tenet 53 (Multi-Source First) is the most recent. Tenets are doctrinally permanent; new tenets only added, never removed.
FCBase
A frozen shipped baseline. Numbered sequentially. Current: FCBase55. Each FCBase is atomic — a full deploy + verification + freeze cycle.
Sealed Prediction
One of the 50 forward-looking statements locked into the predictions ledger on May 17 2026. Cryptographically sealed via SHA-256; reveals at Aug 15 2028 check-in.
Check-in
The scheduled date the predictions ledger updates with actual outcomes. First check-in: August 15 2028. Subsequent check-ins: every 12 months.
