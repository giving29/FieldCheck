Methodology, in the open.

Five dimensions. One composite tier. Disputes published alongside the original rating. No commercial relationship with any rated program. The Morningstar discipline applied to college recruiting.

Five dimensions

Development. Outcome of recruits versus their incoming ranking, aggregated across the coach's career.

Retention. Share who stay versus transfer, last four cycles.

Cultural Health. Assistant turnover, infractions, abuse allegations, transfer-portal feedback patterns.

Promise Delivery. What was sold in recruiting versus what was delivered.

Academic Integrity. NCAA APR, Graduation Success Rate, academic-related infractions.

Composite formula

Wide TrustAll five ≥ 7
Narrow TrustAvg ≥ 6, none < 4
No TrustAny < 4 or avg < 5

Integrity posture

No commercial relationship with any rated program. No paid placements. No methodology consulting. No equity, advisory, or sponsorship deals. We do not accept money from athletic departments, conferences, or recruiting services.

No pre-publication review. Programs do not see ratings before they go live.

Disputes

Programs and players who believe a rating contains factual errors can write to dispute@fieldcheck.ai.

The methodology team reviews within 5 business days. Resolved disputes are published alongside the original rating with the change-log.

Methodology change requests go to methodology@fieldcheck.ai.
