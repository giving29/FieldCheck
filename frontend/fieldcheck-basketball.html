QUEUED · BEHIND FOOTBALL

Basketball, in the queue.

Basketball ships after football. Position taxonomy is the easy part — five positions plus modern hybrids. The harder part: which league anchors the pathway? NCAA → WNBA, NCAA → NBA, NCAA → G League, or NCAA → overseas. We'll surface all four honestly.

§ III · Anchor pipelines

Where the data lives.

Basketball's data landscape is the most developed of any sport we'll cover. Multiple competing combines (NBA + NBPA + Top 100). Two strong pro leagues (NBA and WNBA). Multiple alternate pathways (G League, overseas, NIL-major-college).

// MEN'S · D1 / NBA
The flagship.
362 D1 men's programs. NBA Combine + Draft (60 picks). G League as fallback. Publicly indexed throughout. Strongest data anchor of any sport.
// WOMEN'S · D1 / WNBA
The fastest-growing.
362 D1 women's programs. WNBA Draft (36 picks · increasingly competitive). NCAA Tournament viewership now rivals men's; drafting and recruiting attention follows.
// PHYSICAL PRIORS
The strongest signal.
Wingspan, vertical, 40-foot sprint. More predictive than football combine data because basketball-specific tests map cleanly to game performance. Wingspan especially.
// G LEAGUE / OVERSEAS
The alt-pathway layer.
Unlike volleyball, basketball has multiple viable pro pathways at varying compensation. G League · EuroLeague · Spanish ACB · Australian NBL · NIL-major-college. We'll surface all of them honestly.
// EXCLUDED · TRACKING SERVICES
What we won't use.
247Sports, On3, Synergy paid rankings. All paid commercial products. We'll cite their existence; we won't anchor scoring on them.
// EXCLUDED · NIL
Why we stay out.
NIL deal values are too opaque + inconsistent to anchor pathway predictions. We may surface publicly disclosed deals; we won't use NIL as an archetype input.
§ IV · Archetype skeleton

PG archetypes, first cut.

A first-pass sample. PG is the position most subject to archetype debate; if our framework holds here, it holds everywhere.

// PG · Archetype 1
The Pure Floor General
Pass-first. Decision-making over scoring. Court-vision elite. Career arc anchor: CP3, Stockton, women's: Sue Bird.
// PG · Archetype 2
The Scoring One
Shoot-first PG. Three-level scoring. Volume threat. Career arc anchor: Steph, Damian, women's: Diana Taurasi.
// PG · Archetype 3
The Defense-First One
Defensive specialist on-ball. Pesky, pressuring. Scoring as bonus. Career arc anchor: Avery Bradley, Pat Bev, women's: Briann January.
// PG · Archetype 4
The Hybrid Combo
PG/SG hybrid. Sometimes lead handler, sometimes off-ball threat. Career arc anchor: Kyrie, Trae Young, women's: Sabrina.
