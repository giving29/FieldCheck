Known gaps

The honest things we don't have yet:

  • Pre-D1 international recruits · we miss most non-US prospects until they're listed on a US-school roster. EuroLeague + Asia Pacific cohorts queued for Q3.
  • Olympic-only sports · gymnastics, swimming, track-and-field — visibility windows are too sparse for monthly evaluation. Building a different cadence model for these.
  • D2 / D3 cohorts · intentionally not graded yet. Different roster + ranking systems. Phase 11 add-on.
  • JUCO transfers · partial coverage via transfer portal data. Real depth depends on 247/Rivals JUCO rankings consistency improving.
If we don't have a verdict for someone It's not because they don't matter — it's because we haven't yet had 3 independent sources for all 8 facets. An honest "no verdict yet" is better than a confident wrong one. Athletes get added in cohort batches with PhD review, not one-at-a-time.

How to request a cohort

If you're a coach, parent, or athlete who'd like an evaluation for a specific roster or program, message us through the contact form on any verdict page. Cohort requests are batched monthly and reviewed by the PhD panel for inclusion in the next quarterly drop.
