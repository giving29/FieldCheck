★ THE METHODOLOGY · HOW FIELDCHECK GRADES

The math behind the verdict.

Every grade can be audited. Every voice can be challenged. Every tier band is published. If you want to know why an athlete is rated where they are, the answer is on this page.

01 · THE 8 FACETS

Every athlete is a shape.

Not a single number. Not a Top-100 line. An eight-dimensional polygon that says where the athlete is strong, where they're average, and where the cap is real.

Size
Height, length, frame, leverage — the physical baseline.
Athleticism
Vertical, explosion, change-of-direction, sport-relative power.
Speed
Top speed, first step, reaction time, in-game velocity.
Production
Output that actually shows up in games and matters in context.
Skill
Sport-specific technical mastery — handle, touch, mechanics.
IQ
Read speed, decision quality, film-room signal, anticipation.
Grit
Compete, recovery, motor — the response-to-adversity profile.
Character
Coachability, leadership, team-first signal, off-field profile.

04 · CONFIDENCE BANDS

When we don't know, we say so.

Every FieldCheck verdict ships with a confidence band — not a point estimate dressed up as certainty. A Cooper Flagg verdict might read 9.3 ± 0.2. An early HS prospect might read 6.5 ± 1.4. The width of the band is part of the verdict, not a footnote to it.

If the band is wide enough to cross a tier line, the verdict says so. If the data is too thin to grade, the page says walk away. We never invent precision we don't have.

05 · WHAT WE WON'T DO

The four refusals.

Every other ratings product compromises somewhere. We commit publicly to the opposite.

REFUSAL 01

No payola.

Athletes, agents, schools, leagues — nobody can pay to raise a grade. Not as a sponsorship, not as a partnership, not as a "featured listing." Ever.

REFUSAL 02

No affiliate.

We don't recommend products we earn from. Our verdicts and our revenue never overlap. The recommendation is the product.

REFUSAL 03

No forced verdict.

When the model lacks confidence, the page says walk away. We don't invent precision to fill a page. Empty bands are a feature, not a bug.

REFUSAL 04

No locked truth.

The core verdict is free. Forever. Paid tiers add depth, comps, and trajectories — but the truth about the athlete is on the open page.
