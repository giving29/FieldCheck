// Market — what we sit at the trust layer of

TAM is the trust gap. Not the rankings.

College recruiting and NIL together form a market that has no defensible "trust" infrastructure today. Ranking sites do volume, not accuracy. Family advisors do bespoke, not scale. The trust layer between an athlete + their pathway has been missing — and the gap is widening as NIL deal sizes grow.

$1.6B+
Annual NIL market (2024-25)
Up from ~$900M in 2022-23. Growth driven by collective spend + Power-4 realignment.
~$50M
Annual recruiting media spend
247Sports / Rivals / On3 + emerging platforms. All affiliate-driven.
$0
Spent on auditable trust today
No incumbent offers retrospective accuracy, sourced reasoning, or drift detection. The trust layer doesn't exist.
~$8M
LOVB Pro 2025 collective payroll
Per public reporting. A pro pathway that didn't exist 18 months ago. NIL bands need updating annually.

We compete on a market the incumbents structurally can't enter — because every audit-grade tool we ship makes their ranking model look thinner. The trust layer eats the ranking layer over time. Same way Bloomberg ate trading-floor opinion in finance.
