The Messenger drafts; you send. It never contacts anyone, never touches real contact info, and stores only a privacy-minimal record (who it was about, the read it carried, the date) — never your message or anyone's email.
How this stays honest: the message is grounded in your verdict on The Record — a timestamped, falsifiable number, not a self-promotion. That's the whole pitch: a coach can check the receipt. For coach-to-player outreach, drafts stay strictly professional and route through proper channels — FieldCheck never auto-contacts an athlete.
