// HOW IQ READS AN ATHLETE

Five layers.
Sport-aware. Position-aware.

The same five layers apply across every sport. The attribute names inside them adapt — because Defense in football is not Defense in volleyball, and Pathway means different things for a setter than a quarterback.

01
Physical
Body, athleticism, measurables. Sport-aware attributes inside.
02
Mindset
Resilience. Comeback. Grit. Never-give-up. The intangible the rankings miss.
03
Character
Leadership, coachability, team-first. Sourced from coach + peer record.
04
Pathway
Trajectory and projectability. Where this athlete is going, not just where they are.
05
Defense
Two-way impact. The half of the game the production stat doesn't capture.

Plus one sport-aware production layer per sport: Attacking for volleyball and soccer, Scoring for basketball, Hitting and Command for baseball, Competition for tennis, Tournament for golf, Production for football.
