★ WHAT WE BUILT

Six proof points. Engineered amateur-first.

PROOF 01

110-athlete validation battery.

Continuously-run regression suite: 60 HS · 17 D1 · 13 D2 · 10 D3 · 3 JUCO · 7 pro, spanning 11 evaluation cohorts. Every algorithm change is tested across all 110 athletes before it ships. No other amateur evaluator publishes anything like this.

PROOF 02

HS evidence-ceiling discipline.

High school evidence has a ceiling on what it can prove. Our algorithm caps HS-only composite at 5.40 unless 4-of-4 phenom criteria are met. 247Sports and Rivals routinely grade HS kids at 9+ with no such discipline. We refuse to.

PROOF 03

Cross-level cap discipline.

D2 dominance isn't D1-projectable. HS standout isn't pro-projectable. Test cases live in our regression suite: Dent-Smith (D2-vs-D1 cap), Alozie (HS-to-D1 inverse). The system holds the line.

PROOF 04

Identity-collision protection.

HS namesake matches a famous pro? Most platforms silently inherit the pro evidence into the HS grade. We refuse the merge. Test case in production: Antwan Kimmons / Tim Duncan false-match caught and quarantined.

PROOF 05

One algorithm. Every level.

Tenet 49: same 8 facets, same 16 voices, same Bayesian consensus on HS sophomores and NBA MVPs. Tier bands published: ICON · ELITE+ · ELITE · STAR · PROSPECT · SCOUT. We refuse to bend the framework to fit the level.

PROOF 06

$0 forever for amateurs.

Recruiting services charge families $500–$2,000+ per year. We charge $0 — for athletes, parents, and high school coaches. The verdict on your kid will never be paywalled. This is the structural commitment, not a marketing tier.
