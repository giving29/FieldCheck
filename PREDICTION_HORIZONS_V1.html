Prediction Horizons \u00b7 V1

Internal design \u00b7 June 11 2026 \u00b7 falsifiability in days, not years \u00b7 the learning loop

0 \u00b7 Principle

The 2028 anchor batch stays sealed and untouched \u2014 its immutability is the moat. But proof cannot wait two years. We run a rolling ledger across four horizons so something is ALWAYS resolving \u2014 days away, weeks away, months away \u2014 and every resolution (hit or miss) feeds the calibration battery. The ledger is simultaneously the proof and the training signal.

1 \u00b7 The four horizons

H1
Days \u2013 2 weeks
2026 NBA Draft (June 24\u201325 \u2014 13 days out): draft-range predictions for graded eligibles. MLB Draft (mid-July). Transfer-portal landing spots. HS commitment announcements. Friday line: "our first sealed batch resolves twelve days from this meeting."
H2
Weeks \u2013 months
Season openers: NCAA volleyball + CFB (August), NBA (October), LOVB (January). Starter/role calls, first-month production bands, freshman-impact predictions. Volleyball lane = Kevin-relevant.
H3
One season
All-conference selections, award watchlists, tournament runs, breakout calls from the PROSPECT\u2192STAR ladder \u2014 resolves on a known sports calendar.
H4
Multi-year anchor
The 50 predictions sealed May 17 2026 \u2192 public check-in August 15 2028. Never edited, never replaced. The credibility anchor the copycats cannot retrofit.

2 \u00b7 Sealing mechanism (same discipline at every horizon)

  • Batch JSON: athlete, sport, our composite + tier at seal time, the prediction (range/band/binary), rationale line, resolver (the public event that grades it), resolve-by date.
  • SHA-256 over the canonical JSON + UTC timestamp, recorded in KV (pred:{horizon}:{batch}) and in the repo. Hash published on /predictions the day of sealing \u2014 contents revealed at resolution if competitive-sensitive, or immediately if not.
  • Resolution: outcome recorded next to prediction on /outcomes \u2014 hit / partial / miss, no deletions, no edits.

3 \u00b7 Scoring + the learning loop

  • Per-horizon accuracy scoreboard (hit-rate + calibration error) published on /outcomes \u2014 "we are 7/9 on H1 calls" beats any claim.
  • Every miss becomes a battery case: tagged with which facet/voice mis-read the athlete, fed into the next release's calibration set. Misses are fuel \u2014 this is the Player Thesis engine running at high frequency.
  • Movement Alerts integration: a resolution that shifts an athlete's evaluation emits a real movement event (FCBase83+).
