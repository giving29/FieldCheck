§ 04 · Methodology
InputsEach team carries a per-position depth profile (Elite / Solid / Watch / Gap) calibrated against the published 2026 roster on lovb.com and observed in-season production.
GradeThe overall grade is a weighted average across all five positions. OH and S carry slightly higher weight because they touch more plays per match. Penalties scale with the number of "Gap" positions.
Championship probabilityA simplified heuristic that combines roster grade, position-depth balance, and current standings position. This is not a forecast. It is a directional view on how a championship run might shake out from the snapshot.
Target profilesFor each identified gap, the engine surfaces 1-2 profile types that would address it. These are profile descriptions, not named players. To find named candidates, cross-reference with /lovb/draft (rookies + portal) and /lovb/athletes (current pros).
LimitsDepth profiles are point-in-time and reflect publicly visible information. Internal coaching evaluations may diverge from this read. Re-run after any major roster movement.
